OUR PROCESS

Please Call For All Inquiries

Step 1

Deposits are placed on upcoming litters and are $500. Personal Check or Venmo is Accepted. Puppies are selected on a first-come, first-served basis. Customers who have placed a deposit may choose a puppy in the order their deposit was received.

Step 2

If no puppies are available at the time of your deposit, it will be applied to a future litter.

Step 3

No application is required to make a deposit. Instead, we conduct a “get-to-know-you” phone call with prospective buyers before deposit is made. This allows us to gather all the information we need and get to know our buyers personally.

Step 4

Deposits are non-refundable prior to puppy delivery but are transferable to a future litter if necessary. If you are unable to take a puppy, your deposit may be applied to a future litter. The remaining balance is due at puppy pickup. Cash preferred at time of pickup.

Who is Hoffman's Doodles?

Licensed kennel with 15 years of experience raising healthy, happy, and loving puppies.

Call for Puppy Availability!

Find your new furry best friend today!

All puppies are born and raised in my home, where they are handled and played with daily. I begin housebreaking at around 5–6 weeks old with excellent results—many families compliment how well-trained their puppies are. By the time they go home, puppies are housebroken.

Each puppy comes with a veterinarian health exam certificate and a health guarantee. They are up-to-date on vaccinations and deworming. The puppies’ parents are on-site, and visitors are always welcome to meet them.

I also offer reliable door-to-door ground shipping anywhere in the United States (excluding Alaska, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico). You are welcome to fly in and pick up your puppy as well. International shipping may be arranged on a case-by-case basis.

Puppies are $1,700 + 6% sales tax. A $500 nonrefundable deposit is required to reserve your place on the list. Puppy selection begins at 4 weeks old, and the remaining balance is due at pickup when puppies are 8 weeks old. Please note: no early pickups will be allowed, regardless of request.
